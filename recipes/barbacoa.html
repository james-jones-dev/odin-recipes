Best Barbacoa Recipe

Description

This is the best barbacoa recipe, just like Chipotle. It's great over white rice.

Ingredients

  • Chuck roast
  • Apple cider vinegar
  • Minced garlic cloves
  • Cumin
  • Oregano
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Chicken broth
  • Lime juice
  • Chipotle chiles in adobo

Steps

  1. Combine vinegar, lime juice, chipotles, garlic, cumin, oregano, black pepper, salt, and cloves in a blender or processor on high speed until smooth.
  2. Remove mos of the fat from the roast and then cut into large chunks.
  3. Pour oil into a frying pan and sear all sides of roast on medium-high heat until browned.
  4. Place meat into Crockpot and pour adobo sauce over meat.
  5. Pour in the chicken broth and add bay leaves.
  6. Cook on high heat 6 hours or on low all day.
  7. While still in the Crockpot, shred the meat with two forks and turn the heat to warm. Serve with white rice.
